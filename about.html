OUR MISSION

To equip and build up people to be demonstrators of the knowledge of Christ and His Church-life to the world.

Metamorphoo is God's wisdom to reach unbelievers and unstable new creations to grow in the all-inclusiveness of God.

It's the practical grounds of demonstrating the Church-life to the CHURCH.

At Metamorphoo, everyone is seen as a minister (steward or custodian) of the revelation of Jesus and we complement each other to fulfill God's plans and purposes for our lives.
